Social Stability and Inflation Rates

  • Type: Essay
  • Deadline: W03/S01
  • Points: 15

Description

The essay’s thesis should answer the following question:

  • Is there a symbiotic relationship between social stability and the inflation rate?

Recommended lectures:

  • The Impact of Uncertainty Shocks by Nicholas Bloom (pg. 623 ~ 627).
  • Germany in the Era of Hyperinflation and World War I and the Seeds of Hyperinflation by Alexander Jung.
  • Uncertainty and Hyperinflation by Federal Reserve Bank of San Francisco.
  • Exchange, Prices, and Production in Hyper-Inflation: Germany, 1920-1923 by Frank D. Graham. Section: Results of the currency collapse (pg. 35 ~ 36).
